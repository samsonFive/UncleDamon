A life remembered with love

He had four siblings that preceded him in death, two sisters, Ollie B. Gardner-Boyd and Linda Lamar Gardner and two brothers Arvin and Garrick Juan Gardner. Damon grew up in the 12th Street Area of Detroit. He attended the DPS school systems.
